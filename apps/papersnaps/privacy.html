Privacy Policy

PaperSnaps

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how PaperSnaps collects, uses, and protects your personal information and document data.

Privacy Policy for PaperSnaps

Last Updated: July 2025
App Version: 1.0.0
Company: Sronic LLC

Introduction

Sronic LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our document scanning and management application, PaperSnaps.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Document Images: When you scan or photograph documents, these images are stored locally on your device
  • Document Metadata: Information you add to documents including titles, categories, tags, notes, amounts, and due dates
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, Face ID authentication, currency symbols, and other app configurations

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Location Data: We do not collect or track your location
  • Biometric Data: Face ID authentication is handled by your device's operating system; we do not access or store biometric data

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Document Images: Stored in your device's app documents folder
  • Database: Document metadata is stored in a local SQLite database
  • Settings: App preferences are stored using device's local storage

Data Processing

We process your data to:

  • Display your documents and organize them by categories
  • Enable search functionality across your documents
  • Provide analytics and spending insights
  • Send notifications for document due dates (if enabled)
  • Enable backup and restore functionality

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

No Cloud Storage

  • We do not upload your documents to any cloud services
  • All data remains on your device unless you explicitly share it

Optional Sharing

You may choose to:

  • Share Documents: Use your device's share functionality to send documents to other apps
  • Backup/Restore: Create local backup files that you control and manage
  • Export: Export documents using your device's file sharing capabilities

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for app functionality:

  • Camera Access: To scan and photograph documents
  • Photo Library Access: To save and access document images
  • Face ID: For app authentication (handled by iOS)
  • Local Notifications: To send due date reminders

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • All data is encrypted using your device's built-in encryption
  • Document images are stored in your device's secure app sandbox
  • Database files are protected by your device's security measures

Authentication

  • Face ID authentication is handled by your device's operating system
  • We do not have access to your biometric data
  • Authentication is optional and can be disabled in settings

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View: Access all your documents and settings within the app
  • Edit: Modify document information, categories, and tags
  • Delete: Remove individual documents or all data
  • Export: Create backups of your data
  • Disable Features: Turn off notifications, Face ID, or other features

Data Deletion

  • Individual Documents: Delete specific documents and their associated images
  • All Data: Use the "Delete All Documents" feature in settings
  • App Uninstall: Removing the app will delete all local data

Children's Privacy

PaperSnaps is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Age Verification

  • Users must be 13 or older to use this app
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13
  • If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents/guardians should contact us if they believe their child has used the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice through app updates

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Company: Sronic LLC
Email: privacy-papersnaps@sronic.com
Website: https://sronic.com

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Equal service and price

International Users

If you are using PaperSnaps from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as:

  • You continue to use the app
  • You have not deleted the data
  • The app is installed on your device

When you delete the app or use the "Delete All Documents" feature, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Technical Information

Data Storage Locations

  • iOS: App Documents folder and SQLite database in app sandbox
  • Android: App's private storage directory
  • Backups: User-controlled local files

Data Formats

  • Images: JPEG format for document scans
  • Metadata: SQLite database for document information
  • Settings: Device's SharedPreferences/UserDefaults

Network Usage

  • No Internet Required: App functions entirely offline
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics
  • No Tracking: No third-party tracking or advertising

By using PaperSnaps, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.
