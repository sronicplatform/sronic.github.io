PaperSnaps

Organize your paper invoices, bills & more

Transform your paper clutter into organized digital records. PaperSnaps helps you capture, organize, and manage all your important documents with ease. From invoices to bills, receipts to contracts - keep everything organized and accessible.

Key Features

Smart Document Capture

Capture documents with your camera and automatically extract key information like dates, amounts, and vendor details.

Organized Storage

Automatically categorize and organize your documents by type, date, and vendor for easy retrieval.

Quick Search

Find any document instantly with powerful search capabilities across all your stored documents.

Secure & Private

Your documents are encrypted and stored securely, ensuring your sensitive information stays private.

Cross-Platform Sync

Access your organized documents from any device with seamless cloud synchronization.

Expense Tracking

Track your spending patterns and generate reports to better understand your financial habits.
